# Project Overview Statement (POS)

**Project Title:** UNCW Campus Lost & Found Information System

---

## Problem / Opportunity

Lost and Found operations at UNCW are currently managed through a **manual, staff-driven process** across multiple campus locations. Information related to lost and found items is **distributed across web pages, service desks, and email**, and there is **no single centralized location** where users can search for or report items. As a result, students must visit multiple locations to check for lost items, which creates **delays and inefficiencies**. Staff are required to answer **repetitive Lost & Found inquiries** and track items largely based on **memory**, making consistent and accurate tracking difficult. This presents an opportunity to improve access to information and coordination across campus.

---

## Project Goal

To improve access to campus Lost & Found information by providing a centralized system that enhances visibility and coordination while supporting the existing staff-managed process.

---

## Project Objectives

1. Provide an **online portal** for users to report lost items.
2. Provide a **categorized view of found items** for campus users.
3. Enable staff to **record and manage item details**, including category, color, make/model, and identifying characteristics.
4. Support **status changes** for items (found, transferred, returned).
5. Include **identity verification**, while excluding ownership dispute resolution from project scope.

---

## Success Criteria

1. At least **90% of the defined objectives and requirements** are implemented and validated.
2. Users can access Lost & Found information through a **single centralized system**.
3. Staff can successfully **create, update, and manage item records** within the system.

---

## Assumptions, Risks, and Obstacles

* Staff will continue to maintain **physical custody and security** of all Lost & Found items.
* Lost & Found information will be **managed digitally** within the system.
* Students and campus users will access Lost & Found information **online**.
* **Low staff adoption** may reduce system effectiveness.
* Information may become **out-of-date** if staff do not update entries regularly.
* Display of **PII or sensitive item information** is restricted by institutional rules.
* Once approved, the **project scope is locked**.
